Descriptions in Career page: Job Title: Educational Consultant / Marketing Officer

Company: The ACCITY Ltd
Location: London, UK

Job Overview:

The ACCITY Ltd is seeking a dynamic and results-driven Educational Consultant / Marketing Officer with strong knowledge of the UK higher education landscape (Home and International). The ideal candidate will be confident, well-spoken, and capable of generating and converting leads into successful student enrollments.

Key Responsibilities:
Provide expert guidance on UK higher education opportunities for both Home and International students
Conduct cold calling and outreach to prospective students and partners
Convert inquiries into applications and successfully close daily deals
Build and maintain strong relationships with students, agents, and partners
Promote courses and services through effective marketing and communication strategies
Maintain accurate records of leads, applications, and conversions
Requirements:
Strong understanding of UK education systems, admissions, and student pathways
Excellent communication skills with high proficiency in English
Confident, smart, and well-presented personality
Proven ability in sales, lead generation, and conversion
Ability to work in a fast-paced, target-driven environment
Strong interpersonal and negotiation skills
Preferred:
Experience in education consultancy or student recruitment
Familiarity with UK Home (SFE-funded) and International student processes
