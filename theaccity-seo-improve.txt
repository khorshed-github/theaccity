Step 1: Fix your positioning before doing any technical SEO

Rewrite the homepage and key service pages around the exact service categories you want to rank for:

Study Abroad Consultancy
UK University Admissions
UK Home Student Support
International Student Admissions
Student Finance Guidance
Visa Guidance for Students
SOP / Application / Interview Support

Right now, the homepage headline and sections lean heavily on “visa consulting” language, while the search result snippet already hints at a stronger direction: “international education consultancy” and “UK home and international students.” That education-first wording is much better for your niche and should be used consistently across titles, headings, body copy, and internal links.

Step 2: Do keyword mapping page by page

Do not try to rank one page for everything. Assign one main keyword theme per page.

A practical map for your site:

Homepage: educational consultancy in London / study abroad consultancy UK
About page: trusted study abroad and admissions consultancy
Courses page: UK university courses for international and home students
University partners page: partner universities and colleges in the UK
Contact page: study abroad consultant London / educational consultancy near me
Blog pages: long-tail queries such as student finance UK 2026, UK university intake guide, foundation year in UK, etc.

Google’s SEO Starter Guide recommends making it easy for both users and search engines to understand each page’s purpose.

Step 3: Rewrite title tags and meta descriptions

Your title tags need to be more targeted and commercial.

Examples:

Home title: TheAccity | Study Abroad & UK University Admission Consultants in London
Meta description: Expert guidance for UK home and international students. Get help with course selection, university applications, student finance, SOPs and study abroad admissions from our London team.
Courses title: UK University Courses for International & Home Students | TheAccity
Contact title: Contact TheAccity | Education & Study Abroad Consultants in London.
